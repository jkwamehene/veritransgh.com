About VeriTransGH

Driving sustainable mobility solutions across Africa through innovation, reliability, and commitment to a greener future.

Who We Are

Building the Future of Transport in Africa

VeriTransGH, also known as VGET (Verified Global E-Transport), is more than a website. It is a scalable digital platform built to support individuals, businesses, governments, and transport operators in transitioning to sustainable mobility.

Founded with a vision to revolutionize transportation across Africa, we are committed to building infrastructure that connects communities, reduces carbon emissions, and creates economic opportunities for everyone.

Our platform integrates cutting-edge technology with local expertise to deliver reliable, verified transportation services that meet the unique needs of African cities and communities.

What We Do

Our Core Services

We provide comprehensive solutions for sustainable transportation across multiple platforms and user types.

Passenger App

Book trips, track vehicles in real-time, and enjoy seamless payment options all from your smartphone.

Driver App

Manage bookings, navigate to pickup points, and optimize routes for efficient service delivery.

Station Management

DOP App for station operators to handle check-ins, manage schedules, and monitor operations.

EV Charging Network

Growing network of electric vehicle charging stations across Ghana for sustainable mobility.

Data Analytics

Comprehensive insights for transport operators and government agencies to make informed decisions.

Verified Services

All transport providers are verified and monitored to ensure safety and reliability.
