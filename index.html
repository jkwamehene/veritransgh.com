Process

How Does It Work?

Our streamlined process connects passengers with reliable transport services in three simple steps.

  • Passenger Books

    Customer uses the Passenger App to book a ticket and select their preferred pickup point. Real-time availability and pricing are displayed for transparency.

  • Station Manages

    The Bus Driver App shows booking details and provides navigation to pickup locations. Drivers receive real-time updates and passenger information.

  • Travel with Confidence

    The DOP App handles check-ins, route planning, and station operations. Passengers enjoy a seamless, verified travel experience from start to finish.
